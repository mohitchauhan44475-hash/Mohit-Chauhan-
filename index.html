Connecting You with Verified Local Experts

1. The Problem: The Trust Gap

For Customers

Finding a reliable, nearby plumber, mechanic, or service professional is a headache.

For Professionals

Local experts struggle to find consistent work and visibility.

The Gap

No trusted platform for hyperlocal connection exists.

2. Our Solution: SkillConnect

  • Concept: A GPS-based app connecting customers with verified local experts.
  • Theme: Sky Blue (representing trust and professionalism).
  • USP: Real-time discovery, verified IDs, and transparent ratings.

3. Key App Features

📍 Hyperlocal Search

Find experts within a 5-10km radius instantly.

✓ Verified Badges

Only trusted professionals get the "Verified" tick.

💬 Direct Connect

In-app calling and chat with experts.

⭐ Rating System

High-quality service is rewarded with better rankings.
